Enter your deck list and place the downloaded file in Tabletop Simulator's Saved Objects folder:

  • Windows: My Documents/My Games/Saved Objects/Tabletop Simulator/Saves/Saved Objects/
  • Mac: ~/Library/Tabletop Simulator/Saves/Saved Objects

Formatting examples:

  • 17 Mountain
  • 17 Mountain (IKO)
  • 17 Mountain (IKO) 270
